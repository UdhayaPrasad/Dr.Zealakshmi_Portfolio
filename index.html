ABOUT ME

I am Dr. D. Zealakshmi, a Professor and Head of the Department of Civil Engineering at Royal College of Engineering and Technology. With extensive experience in teaching, research, and academic leadership, I am committed to creating an engaging and innovative learning environment for engineering students. My research interests include advanced concrete technology, structural engineering, and the application of machine learning techniques in civil engineering. I have authored several research publications, conference papers, and academic books focused on improving structural performance analysis and predictive modeling. I am passionate about academic excellence, mentoring students, and contributing to research and interdisciplinary advancements in civil engineering.
